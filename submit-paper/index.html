Submit paper

Follow the instructions given below to submit your paper.

  1. Submit the 'Information form' as the first step (link given below).
  2. Fill the form accurately, and confirm the information with all the authors before submitting it.
  3. For any query regarding the form, mail us at info@techsenary.com.
  4. After submitting the 'Information form, you will be given a unique 'id' for your submission. Note it down for future reference. The 'id' is mandatory for further communication and final submission.
  5. After submitting the 'Information form', the 'Declaration form' should be submitted. To get the 'Declaration form', the 'id' given to you should be submitted in the 'Get Declaration form'. This will ask you to print the declaration form. Print in A4 format.
  6. After printing the 'Declaration form', the signature of the authors should be taken on it, and the respective date and place should be mentioned.
  7. After taking signatures of all the authors mentioned, the clearly scanned copy of duly signed 'Declaration' document should be submitted in the 'Submit Declaration form'.
